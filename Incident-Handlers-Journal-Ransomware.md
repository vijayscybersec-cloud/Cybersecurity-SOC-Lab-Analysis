### The 5 W's of the Incident:
* **Who:** An organized group of unethical hackers known for targeting healthcare and transportation industries.
* **What:** A ransomware attack that encrypted critical medical records and patient data, followed by a demand for a large sum of money for the decryption key.
* **When:** Tuesday morning at approximately 9:00 a.m..
* **Where:** A small healthcare clinic located in the United States.
* **Why:** The attackers gained access via targeted phishing emails containing malicious attachments. Once downloaded, the malware deployed ransomware to disrupt operations for financial gain.
